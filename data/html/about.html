Page Reload Shield Study

The Page Reload Shield study aims to find out why users reload (refresh) web pages and how that is related to page breakage.

The picture below shows what a prompt looks like.

How can I report issues?

You can report issues with this study at the following link: https://github.com/raymak/page-reload/issues

Can I remove this study?

Note that this study is temporary and will be automatically removed after a period of time. If you still want to disable it, click here to see the instructions.

Removing Page Reload
  1. Open the Add-ons page
  2. Find "Page Reload Study 1" and click the "Remove" button
